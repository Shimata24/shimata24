"Tokyo Ghoul" is a Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Sui Ishida. The story follows Kaneki Ken, a college student who becomes a half-ghoul after a chance encounter with one of these flesh-eating creatures. As Kaneki struggles to adapt to his new life, he becomes entangled in the conflict between ghouls and humans.

The series explores themes of identity, morality, and the consequences of prejudice as Kaneki navigates the dark and dangerous world of ghouls and attempts to find his place in society.
