Hitman Reborn

"Katekyo Hitman Reborn!" (often referred to as "Hitman Reborn") is a Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Akira Amano. The story follows Tsunayoshi "Tsuna" Sawada, a timid boy who discovers that he is the heir to the Vongola Mafia family and must train to become its next boss.

Tsuna, along with his quirky guardians, goes through various trials and battles in preparation for his role as the Vongola boss. The series is known for its mix of action, comedy, and character development.
