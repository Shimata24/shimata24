"One Piece" is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the goal of finding the legendary treasure known as One Piece and becoming the Pirate King.

Luffy's journey takes him and his diverse crew, known as the Straw Hat Pirates, to various islands as they encounter powerful foes, make new friends, and explore the vast and adventurous world of the Grand Line.
