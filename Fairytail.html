"Fairy Tail" is a Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Hiro Mashima. The story follows the adventures of Natsu Dragneel and his friends who are members of the Fairy Tail guild, a magical guild known for its chaotic and adventurous nature.

Throughout the series, Natsu and his companions embark on various missions, encounter powerful enemies, and build unbreakable bonds of friendship, all while exploring the magical world of Earth-land.
